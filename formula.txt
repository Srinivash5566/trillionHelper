
### topic: "Number System"
subcategory: "Divisibility Rules"
source: "IndiaBix"
when_to_use: "To quickly check divisibility of integers without full division."
formula: |
  - Divisible by 1 → Always true
  - Divisible by 2 → Last digit ∈ {0,2,4,6,8}
  - Divisible by 3 → Sum of digits divisible by 3
  - Divisible by 4 → Last two digits divisible by 4
  - Divisible by 5 → Last digit ∈ {0,5}
  - Divisible by 6 → Divisible by 2 and 3
  - Divisible by 7 → (Remaining number − 2×last digit) divisible by 7
  - Divisible by 8 → Last three digits divisible by 8
  - Divisible by 9 → Sum of digits divisible by 9
  - Divisible by 10 → Last digit = 0
  - Divisible by 11 → |(sum of odd place digits − sum of even place digits)| divisible by 11
  - Divisible by 12 → Divisible by 3 and 4
conditions_of_use: |
  - Works for base-10 integers
  - Number must be non-decimal
category_tags:
  - number-system
  - divisibility
  - shortcuts

when_to_use: "Area, perimeter, volume, surface area and coordinate geometry problems."
formula: |
  - Triangle area = 1/2 × base × height
  - Triangle area (Heron) = √[s(s−a)(s−b)(s−c)] where s = (a+b+c)/2
  - Area (circle) = πr²
  - Circumference = 2πr
  - Rectangle area = l×b
  - Square area = a²
  - Parallelogram area = b×h
  - Rhombus area = 1/2 × d₁ × d₂

  - Cuboid volume = lbh ; Surface area = 2(lb+bh+lh)
  - Cylinder volume = πr²h ; Curved surface area = 2πrh
  - Cone volume = (1/3)πr²h ; CSA = πrl
  - Sphere volume = 4/3 πr³ ; Surface area = 4πr²
  - Distance between (x₁,y₁) and (x₂,y₂) = √[(x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²]
